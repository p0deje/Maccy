Maccy

Maccy is a clipboard manager for macOS. Open source and free.

Download for macOS

Clipboard manager with no fluff

Maccy does one job - keep you clipboard history at hand. Period.

  • No fluff

    Unlike other clipboard managers, Maccy does only one job - keep your clipboard history and let you access it fast. You won't be overloaded by unnecessary features.

  • Lightweight and fast

    Maccy works blazingly fast. You can open and search your entire clipboard history in just a fraction of s second. Nothing should distract you from what you're focused on.

  • Search-as-you-type

    Just type what you to find in history. You don't need you use your mouse because Maccy is keyboard-first. Don't waste your time moving your hands away from the keyboard.

  • Secure

    Maccy respects your security. If you password manager removes copied passwords from clipboard, so will Maccy.

  • Native

    Maccy is built using native macOS UI and supports Dark Mode. It looks and feels exactly as you'd expect.

  • Open Source and Free

    Maccy is an open source application using MIT license. It is and will always be free.
